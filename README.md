### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness 

**Part A – Case Study**  
I wrote about **Phishing attacks** targeting college students. My case study explains:
- What phishing is
- Step-by-step how it happens
- Who gets targeted (first-year and second-year students)
- Consequences (money loss, identity theft, stress)

**Part B – Prevention Checklist**  
Created a "Stay Safe Online" checklist with 8 tips including:
- Never click suspicious links
- Enable 2FA on all accounts
- Never share OTP or UPI PIN
- UPI safety tips (low transaction limits, avoid random QR codes)
- Reporting channels: cybercrime.gov.in & Helpline 1930
