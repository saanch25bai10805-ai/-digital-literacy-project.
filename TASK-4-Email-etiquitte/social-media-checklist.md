# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

**Do’s**
1. Use a professional profile picture and simple username
2. Keep all posts respectful and positive
3. Enable two-factor authentication on all social media accounts
4. Think before posting — “Will this affect my future internships or job?”
5. Share educational, helpful or achievement-related content

**Don’ts**
1. Never post your personal phone number, address or live location
2. Do not argue, fight or cyberbully anyone online
3. Never share fake news or unverified information
4. Avoid using offensive language, slang or vulgar words in public posts
5. Do not accept friend/follow requests from unknown or random people
