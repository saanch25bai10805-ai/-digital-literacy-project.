Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist for Students

1. Never click on links received in unsolicited emails or WhatsApp messages.
2. Always type the official website address yourself (never click links).
3. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts.
4. Never share your OTP, password or UPI PIN with anyone.
5. Verify UPI payment requests twice before approving.
6. Use only official apps from Play Store / App Store.
7. Keep your phone and laptop software updated regularly.
8. Immediately report any suspicious activity on National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call Helpline 1930 (24×7).

Financial / UPI Safety Tips:
• Set a low daily transaction limit in UPI apps.
• Never scan random QR codes for payments.
